How can I cook spaghetti

  1. Boil water in a larger pot
    • Use at least 4 quarts of water for every pound of noodles
    • put on high heat
  2. Salt the water with at least one tablespoon-- more is fine.
  3. Add the pasta
  4. Stir the pasta
  5. Drain the pasta
